Welcome

Before starting, please make sure the follwing conditions are met:

  • You are using a desktop or laptop computer.
  • You are using a Chromium-based browser. (Chrome, Edge, ...)
  • You have allowed this website to access your webcam.
  • You are in a well-lit environment.
  • Your system's sound is enabled.

Keybinds:

  • In case the window doesnt resize properly, use ctrl+scroll to resize.
  • Press "Enter" to reset audio settings.
  • Press "F" to toggle fullscreen mode.

Quick start:

  • Your hands' position is displayed on screen.
  • Make a fist to grab and drag the inputs.
  • If you only see your camera feed or if hands are not being tracked,
    try refreshing the site (F5).
  • Have fun!
